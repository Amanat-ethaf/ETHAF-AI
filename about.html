About ETHAF AI

ETHAF AI is a multilingual artificial intelligence assistant developed by Amanat. It is designed to help users with learning, research, translation, PDF reading, internet search, navigation, and smart AI-based assistance.

The goal of ETHAF AI is to provide accessible AI technology for students, and everyday users through a simple web interface.

ETHAF AI supports Hindi, English, and Hinglish conversations and continues to improve through learning and memory systems.
